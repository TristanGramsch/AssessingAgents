The assessment process is broken. It outputs regressive tax rolls world wide. Furthering inequality. Burdening those who need a relief. And boosting those who don't need. 

Even the best Assessors Office are regressive. This happens for 2 main reasons: bad data (cheap properties are obscure), and biased exemptions (rich people appeal and win).

AssessingAgents offers a state of the art Assessing workflow. To independently appraise and exempt properties. Producing a fairness seeking tax roll, meant to meaningfully inform the real assessment. The only thing you must do, is feed as much data as possible, everything regarding your assessment and exemption process.

Future work: AssessingAgents starts by deeply understanding your Assessor's Office. Your workflows, your boundaries, your exemption rules, your social conditions, your properties and their sales. AssessingAgents then transforms your data into its own model, combining 3 languages for a complete data representation: maps (Geography Markup Language), properties (Building Information Modeling), and social characteristics. Then, AssessingAgents appraises and exempts properties. Then publishes a tax roll and KPIs (COD, PRD, and sales ratios). AssessingAgents iterates until all tresholds are met. 

In other words, AssessingAgents is an agentic Assessor's office team, realistically simulating appraisal and exemption.
You decide when and were. 

AssessingAgents is a team of 5. 
-Chief assessor: Orchestrator who oversees the team. Talks to the client. Verifies and publishes the tax roll. Educates the public.   
-Assessment data engineer: collects, translates, and prepares data.
-Appraisal learning engineer: investigates, runs experiments, and appraises properties. 
-Exemptions expert: exempts properties based on rules.
